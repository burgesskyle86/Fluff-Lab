# Changelog

## v4.0 — The Laboratory Update

### Added
- Sticky top navigation with Back and Laboratory controls.
- Formula Summary section.
- Required Lab Supplies section.
- Guided Experiment workflow.
- Save & Chill workflow.
- Resume active experiments from Experiment Log.
- Lab Supplies system.
- One current product per generic ingredient.
- Kitchen / Metric measurement toggle.
- Interactive ingredient amount controls with + and - buttons.
- Product catalog with calories, protein, carbs, fat, and fiber.
- Protocol naming during save.
- Protocols preserve the exact products and amounts used.
- Built-in icon set and manifest for Home Screen installation.

### Changed
- Formulas now use generic ingredient names.
- Brand names live in Lab Supplies, not Formulas.
- Default yogurt updated to Siggi's Protein Yogurt.
- Inventory renamed to Lab Supplies.
- Ingredients are grouped into Base, Fruits, Mix-ins, and Flavorings.
- Ingredient amounts are hidden until Make This Formula is selected.

### Fixed / Improved
- Back and Laboratory navigation now remain visible while scrolling.
- Formula browsing no longer automatically starts/saves an experiment.
- Protocols are treated as successful experiments, not just saved recipes.
