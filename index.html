Lab Manual

Use Fluff Lab to build, tweak, and improve high-protein dessert formulas.

Getting Started

  1. Pick a formula from the Formula Builder.
  2. Review the base formula and mix-ins.
  3. Add or remove mix-ins based on what you have.
  4. Use the calories and protein totals to decide if the batch fits your goal.
  5. Save the experiment after you make it.

How Fluff Lab Works

Each formula starts with a repeatable base, then you can add flavor mix-ins. The app tracks calories and protein only so the screen stays clean.

Texture Troubleshooting

  • Too thick: add a splash of milk or let frozen fruit thaw slightly.
  • Too thin: chill longer or use a little more pudding mix next time.
  • Grainy: mix longer before adding delicate toppings.
  • Weak flavor: add extract, citrus juice, or a small crunchy topping.

Nutrition

Calories and protein are based on the ingredient numbers saved in the app. Update them once you lock in the exact labels you use.
