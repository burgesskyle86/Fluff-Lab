Understanding Ingredients

The base usually includes yogurt, protein, whipped topping, pudding mix, and acacia fiber. Mix-ins change the flavor.

Texture Troubleshooting

  • Too thick: add a small splash of milk.
  • Too thin: add a little pudding mix or chill longer.
  • Grainy: mix longer and scrape the sides.
  • Frozen fruit: thaw slightly before mixing.
