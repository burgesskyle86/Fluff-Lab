# Engineering Log

## Decision #001 — Formulas do not contain brands
Formulas use generic ingredients only. Products live in Lab Supplies.

## Decision #002 — One current product per generic ingredient
Each generic ingredient has one active product. The user can change it in Lab Supplies.

## Decision #003 — Protocols preserve history
A Protocol saves the exact products and amounts used at the time the experiment was saved.

## Decision #004 — User names Protocols
The app pre-fills the formula or experiment name, but the user owns the Protocol name.

## Decision #005 — Kitchen measurements are default
Kitchen units are easier for most users. Metric is available as an option.

## Decision #006 — Ingredient amounts appear after Make This Formula
Formula previews show required supplies only. Amounts appear in the Guided Experiment.
